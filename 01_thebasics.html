Ada Lovelace

Who was Ada Lovelace?

Ada Lovelace was born in 1815 and is considered to be the world's first computer programmer.

Quick Facts

Birth/death

1815-1852

Location

England

Interests

Mathematics, logic, and other smart stuff

She was a programmer? But there were no computers in the 1800s!

It's true that there were no computers in the 17th century. However, she applied the logic and anticipated computers of the future. In fact, she predicted that there would be computer-generated music in the future!

She's best known for translating an article on Charles Babbage's Analytical Engine. But she didn't just translate the article--she also wrote her own articles, called her Notes. In there, she talked about her ideas about computers and what they could do.
